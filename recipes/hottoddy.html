Hot Toddy!

This hot toddy is a great evening relaxer when you need something to help relieve that irritating cold.

Ingredients

  • 2 fl oz boiling water
  • 1 1/2 fl oz whiskey
  • 1 tps honey
  • 3 whole cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 slice lemon
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg

Steps

  1. Pour boiling water, whiskey, and honey into a mug. Add cloves, cinnamon, and lemon slice. Let mixture stand for 5 minutes so flavors can mingle, then sprinkle with a pinch of nutmeg before serving
